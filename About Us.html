Our Mission & Values

At H&M Bistro, we say food is not just a meal — it is an experience that brings people together. FROM BUZZ Our restaurant is all about inclusion, sustainability and community. Guaranteed organic, locally-sourced ingredients that benefit local small farmers while reducing our environment-footprint use. We also create a diverse menu that caters to every dietary need, making everyone feel welcomed.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly practices, from sourcing organic ingredients to minimizing food waste. Our commitment to sustainability means you can enjoy a meal that’s kind to the planet.

Inclusivity

Our menu caters to all different diets, from plant-based foods to gluten-free and allergen-free meals. Everybody is invited to our table. All dietary needs are catered for, from plant-based options to gluten-free and allergen-friendly meals. Everyone is welcome at our table.

Community

We are deeply involved in our community and, therefore, partner with the local farmers, artisans, and organizations as a means of building a sense of belonging and positive change.
